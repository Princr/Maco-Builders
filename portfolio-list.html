About Us

MACO BUILDERS, a private limited company by nature of share ownership is a young company and opened for operation in 2006.The company’s operations are in the building and civil engineering sector, mainly construction of buildings, prefabricated and modular housing solutions, plumbing works, repairs and maintenance to existing buildings including minor and major renovations. On civil works, the company specializes in casting of concrete plinths, construction of swimming pools and minor earthworks.
